Why I Chose This

These Book of Mormon scriptures help guide my life, give me strength, and remind me to trust in God daily.

1 Nephi 3:7

I will go and do the things which the Lord hath commanded.

Mosiah 2:17

When ye are in the service of your fellow beings, ye are only in the service of your God.

Alma 37:6

By small and simple things are great things brought to pass.

Ether 12:27

I give unto men weakness that they may be humble, and my grace is sufficient.

2 Nephi 2:25

Men are, that they might have joy.

Moroni 10:4-5

Ask God... and by the power of the Holy Ghost ye may know the truth of all things.
