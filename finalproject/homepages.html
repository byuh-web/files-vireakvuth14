Welcome

Cambodia is a country that has a wonderful culture, history, and natural beauty. Located in Southeast Asia, Cambodia is home to ancient temples, vibrant cities, and unique traditions that have been passed down for generations.

One of the most famous landmarks in Cambodia is Angkor Wat, the largest religious monument in the world. Millions of visitors travel to Cambodia each year to explore its temples, landscapes, and cultural heritag. Learn more from Britannica.
