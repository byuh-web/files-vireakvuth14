Cambodia has a long and rich history that dates back thousands of years. One of the most important periods was the Khmer Empire, which lasted from the 9th to the 15th century. During this time, Cambodia became one of the most powerful civilizations in Southeast Asia. The empire built many impressive temples, including Angkor Wat, which was constructed in the 12th century and is now one of the largest religious monuments in the world.

After the fall of the Khmer Empire, Cambodia went through periods of decline and foreign influence. In the 20th century, the country faced a difficult time during the Khmer Rouge regime. Today, Cambodia has recovered and continues to preserve its cultural heritage while developing as a modern nation. Learn more about Cambodia history from Wikimedia.
